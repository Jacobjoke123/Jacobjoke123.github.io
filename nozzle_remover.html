Coke Nozzle Remover

Nozzle Assembly

Problem:

At work we must take apart the coke machines every night for cleaning. This include removing the nozzles and their internal diffuser. Removing the diffuser from the nozzle is often difficult, time consuming and can result in pain if it is very stuck in the nozzle.

Goal:

Create a simple and easy to produce tool which allows for quick and painless removal of the internal diffuser.
